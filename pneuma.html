Pneuma - The NLP Book Recommendation App

How to Use

  1. Visit the Pneuma app on the web here.
  2. In the provided text box, type in or paste your text expressing your feelings or thoughts.
  3. Click on 'Get Books' to process your entry.
  4. Based on your emotion, the app will detect the underlying emotion and potential ailment, then recommend a set of books for you.

Supported Emotions and Ailments

Currently, the Pneuma app can detect the following emotions:

  • Fear
  • Sadness

Based on the detected emotion, it can predict the following ailments:

  • Fear of Death
  • Fear of Flying
  • Depression
  • Grief

Note: At the moment, the app is specifically coded to detect the above emotions and ailments. Other feelings may not yield accurate recommendations.
